To make pizza dough, combine 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of instant yeast, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and 1 teaspoon of salt in a bowl. Gradually add Â¾ cup of warm water while stirring. Once the dough comes together, knead it on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size. Punch down the dough, divide it into desired portions, and shape them into balls. Let the dough rest for 10-15 minutes before rolling it out for your pizza.
